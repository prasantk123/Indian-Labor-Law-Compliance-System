About Indian Labor Laws
Private Sector Laws:
  • Payment of Gratuity Act, 1972: Provides for gratuity payment to employees completing 5+ years of service
  • Employees' Provident Funds Act, 1952: Mandatory retirement savings scheme
  • Employees' State Insurance Act, 1948: Medical care and cash benefits for employees
  • Factories Act, 1948: Regulates working conditions and leave entitlements
Government Sector Rules:
  • CCS (Pension) Rules: Governs pension and gratuity for central government employees
  • General Provident Fund (GPF): Retirement savings for government employees
  • New Pension Scheme (NPS): Market-linked pension scheme for post-2004 recruits
  • CCS (Leave) Rules, 1972: Leave entitlements for government employees
